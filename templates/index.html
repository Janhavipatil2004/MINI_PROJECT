   Leaf Disease Detection





Leaf disease detection is a critical aspect of modern agriculture and plant pathology, aimed at identifying and managing diseases that affect plant leaves. Leveraging advancements in artificial intelligence and machine learning, automated systems can now analyze images of leaves to detect signs of disease, such as spots, discoloration, or deformities. These systems often utilize convolutional neural networks (CNNs) to process and classify visual data with high accuracy. By providing early and accurate diagnosis, these technologies help farmers and agricultural professionals take timely action to mitigate the spread of diseases, ensuring healthier crops and more sustainable agricultural practices.
